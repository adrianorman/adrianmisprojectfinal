My Hobbies: Exercising, Jiu-Jitsu, Skating

Exercising

I am an active person. Going from participating in three sports throughout high school to having none in college was a tough adjustment. To combat this, I started going to the gym six days a week and running. This hobby is important to me because I want to stay active. I will be participating in the upcoming half marathon for Grandma's Marathon, so this will be a good test of my exercise and endurance.

Jiu-Jitsu

Jiu-Jitsu is important to me because I love combat sports. I have wrestled since I was four years old, so after graduating and no longer wrestling, I needed something similar to do. I joined a Jiu-Jitsu gym because it is similar to wrestling and restored the competitive element that was missing after wrestling.
