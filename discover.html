Discover UMD

Why I Chose UMD

I chose UMD for Duluth's close access to the outdoors and for its strong business school.

What I Like About Duluth

I appreciate Duluth's access to the outdoors. Being able to see Lake Superior from much of the city is a big plus. It has an urban feel but is smaller and more relaxed than Minneapolis.

Experience the vibrant life at the University of Minnesota Duluth.

Personal Experience and Campus Highlights

I enjoy UMD's amenities. The food court is a convenient place to grab a quick meal between classes. I am part of several clubs and serve as president of the Jiu-Jitsu Club. Many buildings are connected, so you rarely need to go outside during the cold winter months. The campus is surrounded by nature; deer are often seen roaming campus, and the Bagley Nature Area is right off campus.
